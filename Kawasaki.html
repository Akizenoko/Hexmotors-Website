KAWASAKI BIG BIKES

Kawasaki is a Japanese multinational corporation primarily known for its motorcycles, heavy equipment, aerospace and defense, and industrial machinery. The company was founded in 1896 by Shozo Kawasaki and has since grown to become one of the largest manufacturers of motorcycles in the world.

Kawasaki motorcycles are known for their performance, reliability, and innovative technology. The company produces a wide range of motorcycles, including sportbikes, cruisers, touring bikes, and off-road bikes. Some of the most popular models include the Ninja series of sportbikes, the Vulcan series of cruisers, and the KLR series of dual-sport bikes.
